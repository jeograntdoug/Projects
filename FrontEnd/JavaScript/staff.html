Our Doctor






Dr. Bill Hanson DVM, DABVP

Dr. Bill graduated from Cornell University with a Bachelor of Science in 1973, and from the Ontario Veterinary College in Guelph with his DVM in 1978. In 1991, Bill successfully wrote his Canine/Feline Specialty exams with the American Board of Veterinary Practitioners (ABVP), recertifying again in 2000 and 2009, qualifying him as a Canine/Feline Specialist until 2021.


Dr. Devon Keys BSc, DC, CAC

Dr. Devon graduated from the University of Guelph with a Bachelor of Science in Human Kinetics and went on to become a Doctor of Chiropractic from the New Zealand College of Chiropractic in Auckland, NZ. She then completed her post-graduate studies with the Veterinary Chiropractic Learning Centre, qualifying her to become an Animal Chiropractor. Her passions with body movement, optimizing structure.
