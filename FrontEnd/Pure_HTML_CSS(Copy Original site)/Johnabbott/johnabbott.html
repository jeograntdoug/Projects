CAFETERIA

Located in Stewart Hall, the Cafeteria is open Monday to Thursday, 7:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., and Friday, 7:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., and serves hot nutritional meals (breakfast and lunch) at reasonable prices. The Cafeteria includes a salad bar, self-serve soup counter, a panini sandwich counter and a self-serve international or pasta bar.

This space is equipped with microwaves, electrical outlets, and a phone charging station.
